5.381 DBA_STATEMENTS

DBA_STATEMENTS Statements in stored objects accessible to sys. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_STATEMENTS.

Note:

This view is available starting with Oracle Database 12c Release 2 (12.2.0.1).
