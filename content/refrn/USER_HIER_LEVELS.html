6.439 USER_HIER_LEVELS

USER_HIER_LEVELS describes the levels of the hierarchies owned by the current user.

Its columns (except for OWNER) are the same as those in ALL_HIER_LEVELS.

Note:

This view is available starting with Oracle Database 12c Release 2 (12.2.0.1).
