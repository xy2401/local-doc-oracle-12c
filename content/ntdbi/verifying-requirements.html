Verifying Requirements

Before you try any of the troubleshooting steps, do the following:

  • Check to ensure that the system meets the requirements and that you have completed all of the preinstallation tasks.

  • Read the release notes for the product on your platform before installing it. You can find the latest version of the release notes on the Oracle Technology Network website:

    http://docs.oracle.com
